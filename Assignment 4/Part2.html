I want to make sure the order of the form makes sense and flows relatively easily with proper labelling to go along with it. Another is to make sure that the form is completely compatabile with older browsers as well to ensure that anyone and everyone has access to it. There are a ton of ways to make filling out a form easier for the user, such as auto-filling and error warnings that pop up as soon as possible. Formatting should be straightforward, give an example of what you're looking for but make the browser automatically format input without the user having to do it themselves.
